Frequently Asked Questions
Question Answer
Are dogs allowed within the park? No. They are allowed after 7pm, however
when does the park open for the year? Memorial day weekend
When does the park close for the year? Labor day
What time does the park open? 8am on weekends, 9am on weekdays
What time does the park close? 7pm every day
Does the park close for weather Park stays open during operating hours no matter the weather
Is alcohol allowed in the park? Yes. Glass is prohibited, however.
Are there trash cans/garbage removal? No. All State parks are carry in/carry out.
Can I park on the beach road? Only to load/unload vehicles. Parking is restricted to the lots.
Is there handicapped parking? Yes, on the left side of the beach road.
Can I park on the Dam and walk down? No. Entry to the park must be done through the main entrance.
Can I leave the park, and come back? Yes, just present your tickets to the booth attendant.
Are boats allowed on the lake? Yes, but gas motors are prohibited.
where can I launch my boat? Boat launch within the park.
Can I bring my ATV, dirtbike? No. The trails cannot be accessed from the park.
