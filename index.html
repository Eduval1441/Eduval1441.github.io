Why Come To Clough

  • Swimming
  • Fishing
  • Boat Launch
  • Charcoal Grills
  • Rentable Pavilion
  • Rentable kayaks/canoes
  • Large dam to explore
  • Hiking trails

Clough State Park is located in beautiful Weare NH right next to the Everett Dam. Clough features a 900 foot beach that comes equipped with charcoal grills, picnic tables and a rentable pavilion. Kayaks and canoes can also be rented for $15 an hour from 10am to 4pm. Clough State Park also has a boat launch and newly blazed hiking trails.

To learn more about the park, call the number listen on the index page!
